How to Get Combination Range

HaiyueOctober 22, 2024About 2 minmath
On This Page
  • Background
  • Solution

Background

There are three variables, name target,candidate and others,

  • The range of target is [a,b][a, b][a,b].
  • The range of candidate is [c,d][c, d][c,d].
  • The range of others is [e,f][e, f][e,f].

There is a relationshp: target=candidate+otherstarget = candidate + otherstarget=candidate+others
Now give the range of target [x1,x2][x1, x2][x1,x2], to find the available combination for candidate and others.

Solution

  • Step 1: Find the value of candidate v∈[c,d]v \in [c, d]v∈[c,d].

  • Step 2: [e+v,f+v]∩[x1,x2]≠∅[e+v, f+v] \cap [x1, x2] \neq \empty[e+v,f+v]∩[x1,x2]=∅

  • Step 3: max(e+v,x1)≤min(f+v,x2)max(e+v, x1) \le min(f+v, x2)max(e+v,x1)≤min(f+v,x2)

  • Range 1:

  • Range 4:
    {v≤x2−ev≥x1−ev≥x2−fv≥cv≤d \begin{cases} v &\le x2-e \\ v &\ge x1-e \\ v &\ge x2-f\\ v &\ge c \\ v &\le d \end{cases}⎩⎨⎧​vvvvv​≤x2−e≥x1−e≥x2−f≥c≤d​ ⇒\Rightarrow⇒ v∈[max(x1−e,x2−f,c),min(x2−e,d)]v \in [max(x1-e, x2-f, c), min(x2-e, d)]v∈[max(x1−e,x2−f,c),min(x2−e,d)]

  • Step 5:

    1. select a value v in available ranges for candidate,
    2. update date target to [x1-v, x2-v]
    3. select one element from others as candidate and remove it from others
    • if others is not empty,
      4. update others as the sum of others elements (except new candidateelement)
      5. Go to Step 1.
    • else go to Step 6.

  • Step 6:
    select a random value from the intersection of target and candidate.
