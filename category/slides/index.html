      What's data stream

      A data stream is a real-time, continuous, ordered (implicitly by arrival time or explicitly by timestamp) sequence of items. It is impossible to control the order in which items arrive, nor is it feasible to locally store a stream in its entirety.

      07.Geospatial(W10&11)

      Geospatial questions

      Spatial Question Description
      Location
      (identify)      		 What exist at specific location?
      What object is located at 49N 123W?
      Condition
      (search)      		 Where can you find specific conditions?
      where do low income residents live?
      Trends
      (overlay analysis)      		 What has changed over time?
      What is the change in green area size from 2000?
      Routing
      (network analysis)      		 Which is the best way to travel?
      How can I reach a destination in the least time?
      Patterns
      (Query analysis)      		 How are object distributed in geographic space?
      Does salmon streams affect bear presence?
      Modeling
      (building spatial models)      		 How would conditions change in the future?
      What areas will be floored by 1m sea level rise?
